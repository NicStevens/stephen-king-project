Why is Stephen King my favorite author?

Stephen King is my favorite author because his stories entertain me, thrill me, and keep me wanting to know what happens next. Known as the "King of Horror," he is a master of writing chilling horror stories as well as skilled at writing suspense, crime, science fiction, fantasy, and crime. In addition to being an award winning author with a unique imagination, King and his wife, Tabitha King, give back to communities by contribute to many local and national charities.
