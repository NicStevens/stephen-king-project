Why is Stephen King my favorite author?

Stephen King is my favorite author because his stories entertain me, thrill me, and keep me wanting to know what happens next. Known as the "King of Horror," King is a master of writing chilling horror stories as well as skilled at writing suspense, crime, science fiction, and fantasy. In addition to being an award winning author with a unique imagination, King and his wife, Tabitha King, give back to communities by contributing to many local and national charities.

|Movies

Movie adaptations of my favorite Stephen King books

The Mist

This film is about a freak storm that hits a small town and brings with it a mist that contains something terrifying and deadly. A small group of citizens trapped in a supermarket must fight for their lives.

Release date

November 21, 2007

What I like about it

The Mist brings together horror and science fiction. It is scary, exciting, engaging, and contains surprising moments.

View trailer

The Shining

The Shining is about a family staying at an isolated hotel for the winter where a sinister presence influences the father into violence and his psychic son sees horrifying visions.

Release date

May 23, 1980

What I like about it

The Shining is frightening and entertaining. Masterfully acted, it keeps you on the edge of your seat wondering how it's going to end.

View trailer

Stand By Me

Based on Stephen King's novella "The Body," this film is about a writer who tells the story of a childhood journey with his friends as they try to find the body of a missing boy.

Release date

August 22, 1986

What I like about it

Stand By Me is a classic film about friendship. With a great cast, it is a fun watch with poignant moments about life and growth.
